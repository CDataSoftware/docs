## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Smartsheet to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Smartsheet* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Select the Authentication method, then proceed to the relevant section and follow those instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Authentication Methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="PersonalAccessToken">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter your **Personal Access Token**. You can get your token in your Smartsheet Account by navigating to **Account > Personal Settings > API Access** and using the provided form to generate it.
</Step>
<SaveAndTest/>
</Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="OAuth">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter the **OAuth Client Id** assigned when you registered your Smartsheet account for OAuth.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **OAuth Client Secret** for your Smartsheet account for OAuth.
</Step>

</Steps>
