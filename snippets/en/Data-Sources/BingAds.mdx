## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Bing Ads to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Bing Ads* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter your **Customer Id**. You can find this Id by logging into your Bing Ads account and navigating to the **Accounts and Billing** settings page. Your customer Id also appears as the final number in the Bing Ads URL when you are logged in (for example, `azure.bingads.microsoft.com/cc/AccountDetails?cid=123456789`).
</Step>
<Step>
Enter your **Developer Token**. To get the token, log in to the [Bing Ads Developer Portal](https://developers.ads.microsoft.com/Account) as a Microsoft Account user with the **Bing Ads Super Admin** role. Then, click **Request Token**.
</Step>
<Step>
(Optional) Enter your **Account Id**. This can be found in your Bing Ads account by navigating to the **Accounts and Billing** settings page. Then, click **Your Account**. If not specified, the connection automatically queries the first account found.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Bing Ads sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Bing Ads account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>

</Steps>
