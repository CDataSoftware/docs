## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Square to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Square* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
(Optional) To set a business location as default, enter the Id in the **Location Id** field. Otherwise, leave this field blank.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Square sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Square account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>

</Steps>
