## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect SharePoint to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *SharePoint* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the base **URL** for the SharePoint site (for example, `https://teams.contoso.com`).
</Step>
<Step>
Select the **Share Point Edition** from the drop down.
</Step>
<Step>
Select the Authentication method, then proceed to the relevant section and follow those instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Steps>
<Step>

</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the SharePoint sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your SharePoint account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
