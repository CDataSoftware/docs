## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect MYOB to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *MYOB* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Select your **Instance**.
</Step>
<Step>
In **User**, enter the MYOB username for authentication.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the user password into the **Password** field.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **Company File Id**. If not specified, the ID of the first returned company file is used.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the MYOB sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your MYOB account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>
