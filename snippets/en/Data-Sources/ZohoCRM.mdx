## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Zoho CRM to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Zoho CRM* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Zoho CRM sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Zoho CRM account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>
<Step>
Select the CRM organization that you want to connect to Connect AI and click **Submit**.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Accept** to grant the requested access permissions. **Note:** Each permission is required for the connection to function.
</Step>
