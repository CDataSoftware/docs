## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Exact Online to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Exact Online* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Select a region in the **Region** drop-down list that is associated with your Exact Online administration.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Exact Online sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Exact Online account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>
<Step>
The **Division** field populates with the divisions associated with your Exact Online administration. Select a division from the drop-down list. The default value is the first division in the list.
</Step>
<SaveAndTest/>
</Steps>
