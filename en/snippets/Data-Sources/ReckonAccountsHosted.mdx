## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Reckon Accounts Hosted to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Reckon Accounts Hosted* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter your Reckon Accounts Hosted **User** name.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter your Reckon Accounts Hosted **Password**.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the complete path of where your **Company File** is located, for example, *Q:/MyCompanyFile.qbw*. The company file usually has a `.qbw` or `.qba` extension.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **Country Version** of Reckon Accounts Hosted, for example, *2018.R2.AU*.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Reckon Accounts Hosted sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Reckon Accounts Hosted account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>
