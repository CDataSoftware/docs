## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Facebook Ads to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Facebook Ads* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Facebook Ads sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Facebook Ads account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>
<Step>
The **Account Id** field populates with your account Ids. Select an account Id from the drop-down list. The default value is the first account Id.
</Step>
