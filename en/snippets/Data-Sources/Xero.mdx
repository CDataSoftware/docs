## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Xero to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Xero* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Select the Xero **Schema** to connect to.
</Step>
<Step>
(Optional) Set a Tenant Id to connect to. If left blank, the connection will use the first available tenant.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Connect to Xero** to open the Xero sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Xero account with your email address and password.
</Step>
