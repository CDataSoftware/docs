## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Jira to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Jira* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **URL** to your Jira endpoint.
</Step>
<Step>
Select the Authentication method, then proceed to the relevant section and follow those instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Step>

</Step>
<SaveAndTest/>

  <Tab title="Crowd">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter the Jira username for authentication in the **User** field.
</Step>
<Step>
In the **Password** field, enter the user password.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **SSO Login URL** associated with the Crowd account. You can find the IDP URL by navigating to **your application > SSO > SSO information > Identity provider single sign-on URL**.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **SSO Exchange URL** used to exchange the SAML token for Jira cookies. This URL can have the following format: `https://<authority of Jira instance>/plugins/servlet/samlsso`.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **SSO App Name** of the application.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **SSO App Password** of the application.
</Step>
<SaveAndTest/>
</Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="OKTA">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter the Jira username for authentication in the **User** field.
</Step>
<Step>
In the **Password** field, enter the user password.
</Step>
<Step>
Set the **SSO Login URL** to the URL used by the SSO provider.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter all relevant property-value pairs into the **SSO Properties** field with the format `ssoproperty1=value1;ssoproperty2=value2;ssoproperty3=value3;`. Make sure to separate all property-value pairs with semicolons.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **SSO Exchange URL** used to exchange the SAML token for Jira cookies. This URL can have the following format: `https://<authority of Jira instance>/plugins/servlet/samlsso`.
</Step>
