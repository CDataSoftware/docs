## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Oracle Eloqua to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Oracle Eloqua* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Select the Authentication method, then proceed to the relevant section and follow those instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Authentication Methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter the **Company** name on the Oracle Eloqua account.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the Oracle Eloqua username for authentication in the **User** Field.
</Step>
<Step>
In **Password**, enter the user password.
</Step>
<SaveAndTest title="Oracle Eloqua"/>
</Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="OAuth">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter your **OAuth Client Id**.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **OAuth Client Secret** for that Id.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Oracle Eloqua sign-in page in a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Oracle Eloqua account and provide the requested permissions (if applicable).
</Step>
