## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Gmail to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Gmail* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Confirm your **Schema** in the drop down.
</Step>
<Step>
Select the Authentication method, then proceed to the relevant section and follow those instructions.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Authentication Methods

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="OAuth">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter the **OAuth Client Id** assigned when you registered your Gmail account.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the **OAuth Client Secret** for your Gmail account.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **Sign in with Google** to connect securely through OAuth. This action opens the Gmail sign-in page on a new tab.
</Step>
<Step>
Log in to your Gmail account.
</Step>
<SaveAndTest/>
</Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="OAuthJwt">
<Steps>
<Step>
Enter your **OAuth JWT Cert**. This is a multi-line key.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter your **OAuth JWT Subject**. This must be set to the email address of the user whose data you want to access. The account must also be part of a GSuite domain.
</Step>

</Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
