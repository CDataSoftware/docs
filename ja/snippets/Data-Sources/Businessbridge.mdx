## Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect Business b-ridge to your Connect AI account:

<Steps>
<Step>
Open the **Connections** page of the Connect AI dashboard.
</Step>
<Step>
Click **\+ Add Connection** in the upper-right corner.
</Step>
<Step>
Type *Business b-ridge* into the search field, then click the data source name.
</Step>
<Step>
On the **Basic Settings** tab of the new connection, enter a connection name or keep the default name.
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the required fields for authentication: **Company Key**, **Subscription Key**, and **Project Key**. To obtain the subscription key, log in to the Business b-ridge API portal and select **Your name** > **Profile**. In the subscription section, click **Main Key**. 
</Step>
<Step>
Enter the URL of your Business b-ridge account (optional). The URL is in the following format: `https://{account}.azure-api.net`.
</Step>
